Orange Geranium & Otis

Orange Geranium & Otis are a dynamic duo. Formed after the Great Floral Shop Robbery of 1997, these two warriors of justice fight crime, one seed and one flower at a time. Orange Geranium is a florist by day but a hero by night, who goes by the name Orion Green. Otis is Octavian Sigmund, who is a daytime vet and Orange's animal-loving sidekick. Both have dedicated their lives to fighting crimes against the environment.

Orange Geranium can:

  • Spawn flowers anywhere, namely geraniums
  • Can fight with whip-like vines

Otis can:

  • Can talk to animals using telepathy
