My favorite food is spaghetti!

My favorite food is spaghetti, especially homemade spaghetti! Ever since I was young, spaghetti has always been a staple dish. You can make spaghetti with meatballs or without, but for me, meatballs are a must. On a cold winter's day or a long day at work or school, spaghetti is the perfect dish to have for dinner!
